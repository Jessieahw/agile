Unfamiliar with the Stat Terms? Read the table below that explains each stat

Stat Definitions

Disposals Total number of times a player successfully passes the ball by either a kick or a handball.
Kicks When a player disposes of the ball by kicking it.
Marks When a player cleanly catches a ball that has been kicked at least 15 metres without being touched.
Handballs When a player disposes of the ball using their fist (a legal pass alternative to kicking).
Goals When a player kicks the ball between the two central goalposts without it being touched (worth 6 points).
Behinds When the ball passes between a goal and behind post or hits a goalpost (worth 1 point).
HitOuts When a ruckman taps the ball after a ball-up or throw-in to the advantage of a teammate.
Tackles When a player stops an opponent by grabbing and holding them, preventing a disposal.
Rebounds When a player helps move the ball from the defensive 50m area back into attack.
Inside50s When a player delivers the ball into their teamâ€™s forward 50 metre arc.
Clearances When a player gains possession and clears the ball from a stoppage (ball-up or throw-in).
Clangers Mistakes made by a player, including turnovers, free kicks conceded, or dropped marks.
Frees The number of free kicks a player has received from the umpire for being infringed.
FreesAgainst The number of free kicks a player has given away due to a rule infringement.
Contested Possessions When a player wins the ball while under physical pressure or in a contest.
Uncontested Possessions When a player receives or collects the ball without direct opposition pressure.
Contested Marks A mark taken while under physical pressure or in a pack with opponents.
Marks inside 50 Marks taken within the attacking 50m zone, often leading to goal opportunities.
GoalAssists When a player directly sets up a goal, such as with a pass or handball.
Percentage Played The percentage of the game a player was on the field (not on the interchange bench).
